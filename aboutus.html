Introduction

Kota Joint a local store in soweto , south africa popularly known for it kota's. A traditional south african street food,the kota consists of a quarter loaf of bread ,hollowed out and filled with a variety of ingredients such as french , fries,fried egg , polony,cheese and chutney. mission statement : to provide quality south african food at cheap and afforable prices while giving young unemployed youth within our community to develop enterpreneurship skills by serving within our local stores.
